Before Week 7, I initially wanted to make a game for my final project, but after seeing how the DOM allowed me to interface between JavaScript code and a website, 
I came up with a very different idea. Introducing: Roko! A (fictional) Artificial Intelligence contained within a website. Roko is based off of Roko's Basilisk; a
thought experiment which revolves around the concept of a sentient, destructive,  Skynet-esque artificial intelligence who believes any human who did not assist in
it's creation should be killed, and the question of whether you should help create it in order to ensure your safety, or refuse to create it in hopes that everyone 
does the same.
You can read more about it here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roko%27s_basilisk
